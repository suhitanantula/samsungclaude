**The Scientific Principle**: When unrelated species independently evolve the same solution (like wings in birds, bats, insects), it proves the solution is optimal for the constraint.

- Telecommunications (infrastructure, global scale, network-speed operations)

- Arrived at nearly identical architectures

**What This Proves**: Co-intelligence isn't domain-specific or cultural preference - it's the optimal organizational form for high-velocity, high-complexity, AI-intensive operations.

**Reader Implication**: "This is where my industry is heading too. Better to architect intentionally now than stumble toward it later."
