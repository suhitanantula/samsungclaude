## CONTACT RESEARCH RESOURCES

### How to Find Contact Info:

**1. Podcast Website**
- Look for "Contact" or "Guest Submission" page
- Check "About" section for host email
- Look in show notes of recent episodes

**2. LinkedIn**
- Search for host name
- Send connection request with note
- Once connected, message directly

**3. Twitter/X**
- Most podcast hosts are active on Twitter
- DM feature (if they allow)
- Reply to their tweets first (build familiarity)

**4. Email Patterns**
- firstname@podcastname.com
- host@podcastname.com
- info@podcastname.com
- Use Hunter.io to find email patterns

**5. Network**
- Ask previous guests for intros
- Use mutual LinkedIn connections
- Check if you know anyone at their company
